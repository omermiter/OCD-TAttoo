I.  Before your appointment Read once, keep at hand
I.
Sleep well the night before.Tired skin takes ink badly.
II.
Eat a full meal two hours before.Bring a snack for long sessions.
III.
No alcohol for 24 hours.It thins the blood and affects healing.
IV.
Avoid sun on the area for two weeks before.Sunburnt skin cannot be tattooed.
V.
Moisturise the area daily in the week before.Healthy skin holds pigment better.
VI.
Wear loose, dark clothing over the placement area.Ink may stain lighter fabrics.
VII.
Hydrate — start the day before.Water helps the skin accept pigment.
VIII.
Arrive a few minutes early, calm.Bring headphones if long sessions suit you.
II.  After your appointment Your healing guide
I.
Keep the wrap on for 2–4 hours, then remove gently.If a second-skin film was applied, leave it 3–5 days.
II.
Wash with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap, twice daily.Pat dry — never rub.
III.
Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturiser 2–3 times a day.Less is more — the skin needs to breathe.
IV.
Do not pick, peel, or scratch as it heals.Pulling scabs pulls ink.
V.
Avoid direct sun for at least 4 weeks.After that, SPF 50+ is a lifetime rule.
VI.
No swimming, saunas, or soaking for 3–4 weeks.Submerging prolongs healing and risks infection.
VII.
Expect some redness, peeling and itching in week one.This is normal — resist the itch.
VIII.
Full skin healing takes 4–6 weeks; deep layers up to 3 months.Be patient — the colour will settle beautifully.
